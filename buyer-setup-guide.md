# Nourish — Buyer setup guide

## What this product is

**Nourish** is a **private web app** for pregnancy nutrition support—areas to focus on, meal logging, and gentle guidance tailored to your trimester. One page, no app store install.

This is a **private web app. No login, no subscription.** Your entries save **locally in your browser** on the device you use.

---

## How to open the hosted app link

Use the HTTPS URL from your seller (for example):

`https://your-name.github.io/your-repo-name/`

Open in **Safari** (iOS) or **Chrome / Safari / Firefox** (desktop).

1. Open the link in **Safari**.

---

## Use on desktop

Bookmark the hosted page or open **index.html** from your downloaded package in a desktop browser.

---

## Privacy & local storage

**This is a private web app. No login, no subscription.** Your entries save **locally in your browser** on the device you use.

### Important warning

**If you clear browser data or switch devices, export a backup first.**

---

## Backup & restore (export / import)

### Export

Use the app’s **Export backup** option. Downloaded files are typically named **`nourish-backup.json`**. Keep multiple copies if your entries matter to you.

### Import

Use **Import backup** and select a **`nourish-backup.json`** (or compatible export) from this app. Read confirmation dialogs—import may replace existing local data.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | What to try |
|--------|----------------|
| Fonts look wrong offline | Ensure you deploy **index.html** intact; fonts are embedded in the file. |
| Export missing | Allow downloads in browser settings; try desktop Chrome if mobile blocks saves. |
| Old data after reinstall | Import your latest **`nourish-backup.json`**. |
| Storage errors | Free disk space; avoid full Private/Incognito-only use for long-term logs. |
